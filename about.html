The Mission

There's a whole world of genuinely good watches hiding under $50, and a lot of junk too. Every week I buy one, wear it, and rate it honestly so you know what's worth your money before you spend it.

How We Rate

Every watch gets scored 1–5 stars across five categories:

  • Look — design, dial, presence on the wrist
  • Feel — build quality, comfort, materials
  • Accuracy — how well it actually keeps time
  • Water Resistance — real-world durability
  • Value — is it worth the price?
